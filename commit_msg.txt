Refactor cmd_panel polling and fix RTC infinite loops

- Replaced indefinite `while(rtc_is_updating())` loops with 100ms timeout
  using `hal_timer_ticks()` in `kernel/drivers/rtc/rtc.c`.
- Replaced non-atomic `sti; hlt` polling sequences with `hal_cpu_enable_interrupts_and_halt()`
  and updated raw asm `cli`/`sti` calls to use `hal_interrupts_disable`/`enable()` in `cmd_panel.c`.
- Included missing `mock_hal.c` to `test_rtc` in CI scripts.
